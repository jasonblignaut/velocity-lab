Lab Prerequisites

Before starting your 4-week journey, ensure you have the following requirements in place:

Cloud Services

  • Microsoft 365 free trial from microsoft.com
  • Access to Entra ID and Exchange Online (included in trial)

Hardware Requirements

  • Old laptop with minimum 8GB RAM and 100GB disk space
  • VirtualBox installed from virtualbox.org
  • Bridged networking configured (e.g., 192.168.1.x subnet)

Network Setup

  • Static IP from your ISP (~R25/month via Afrihost or Telkom)
  • Firewall ports opened: 25 (SMTP), 587 (SMTPS), 443 (HTTPS), 993 (IMAPS)
  • Port forwarding configured to laptop's IP address
  • Stable internet connection (minimum 10 Mbps)
