Timeline Guide to Implement the Lab

Welcome to Velocity Lab! Please register or log in to track your progress through the four-week lab timeline.

Lab Prerequisites

  • Create a Microsoft 365 free trial at microsoft.com.
  • An old laptop (minimum 8GB RAM, 100GB disk) for lab exercises.
  • Install VirtualBox from virtualbox.org.
  • Request a static IP from your ISP (~R25/month).
  • Open firewall ports 25, 587, 443, and 993 on all servers and router.
  • Ensure stable internet (minimum 10 Mbps).
